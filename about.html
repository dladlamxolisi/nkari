About Us

The laboratory has been established in Johannesburg and aims to provide high quality - cost-effective diagnostic tests.

The practice is a level 1 BBBEE contributor and complies with all the statutory requirements set by the Health Professions Council of South Africa.

We only use the latest equipment from reputable suppliers to ensure that the results produced are of the highest quality. All laboratory professionals are highly skilled and kept abreast of the latest technological advances in the field to perform technical and phlebotomy tasks.

Our philosophy is to engage with our clinicians and educate our patients.

We are diversely proud in our approach to new advancements and ensure a thorough path to diagnostic accuracy.
