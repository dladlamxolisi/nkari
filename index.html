About Us

The laboratory has been established in Johannesburg and aims to provide high quality - cost-effective diagnostic tests.

The practice is a level 1 BBBEE contributor and complies with all the statutory requirements set by the Health Professions Council of South Africa.

We only use the latest equipment from reputable suppliers to ensure that the results that are produced are of the highest quality. All laboratory professionals are highly skilled and kept abreast with the latest technological advances in the field to perform technical and phlebotomy tasks.

Our philosophy is to engage with our clinicians and educate our patients.

We are diversely proud of our approach to new advancements and ensure a thorough path to diagnostic accuracy.

Our Services

Corporate Wellness

We offer a wide variety of screening tests for employees of corporates. Enquire about our testing profiles as well as additional monitoring testing that can be customized to your needs.

Genetics

Our lab uses the latest techniques to analyze biological markers in the genome and proteome of samples. We offer DNA tests in the form of paternity, maternity as well as family relationship studies.

Microbiology

Analysis is done on a number of specimens including urine, stool, sputum, blood, CSF, and swabs from various infected tissues. Results are available from 24 hours onwards depending on pathogens isolated.

Biochemistry

The analysis of bodily fluids mainly serum and plasma. We use hi-tech equipment to analyze for chemicals present in the blood, e.g. blood sugars, lipids, liver functional, enzymes, and hormones.
